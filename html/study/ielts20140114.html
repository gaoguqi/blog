Education vocabulary

 discipline 学科，纪律，训练
punctuation标点
Your initial discipline is your vocabulary, 
then grammer and punctuation and finally the courage to simply everything.

Cultivate 培养，陶冶，耕作
cultivate independence 培养独立性, cultivate patience 培养耐心 
If you cultivate an attitude, image, or skill, 
you try hard to develop it and make it stronger or better.
If you cultivate land or crops, you prepare land and grow crops on it. 
foster 培养，养育 vt 收养的adj
foster parents 养父母 foster father养父
foster an enterprise culture of safety and security. 培养安全可靠的企业文化
foster children 收养孩子
nurture 养育，鼓励，培养
nurture curiosity kjʊərɪ'ɒsɪtɪ 先读curi然后再念osity 培养好奇心 curiosity
Parents want to know the best way to nurture and raise their children to adulthood.
adulthood成年 adult hood分开念就可以
If you nurture plans, ideas, or people, you encourage them or help them to develop.
She has always nurtures great ambitions for her son. 
ambition雄心壮志 重音在b 她一直在培养儿子的雄心壮志
The human organism('ɔrɡənɪzəm 有机体) learns partly by nature, partly by nurture. 
人的学习能力部分考天生，部分靠后天培养

promote the students' physical,mental
(or intellectual ɪntə'lektʃʊəl 一样是分开念inte llectual 智力的聪明的)
and emotional （情绪的） development 促进学生身心发展
soundness 健康稳固完整
psychological saɪkə'lɒdʒɪk(ə)l 分开念psycho logical 
psychology 则不同重音在ch， psy chology分开念
psychological soundness/well-being/welfare 心理健康

apt恰当的，有...倾向的 aptitude 先天学习能力
He discovered an aptitude for working with accounts. 
他发现自己有管账的天资。
talent 才能天赋
She is proud that both her children have a talent for music/maths
后天学习能力用skill技巧技能
I have both skill and dash.
我既有技巧，又有闯劲
The cut of a diamond depends on the skills of its craftsman.
切割钻石的好坏取决于工匠的手艺。

Give the students motivation to do sth.
 Motivate the students to do something.

adolescent ædə'les(ə)nt 青春期的青少年 ado 然后重音读lescent

It is important that an adolescent boy should have an adult in whom he can confide.

impart 给予，传授
The ability to impart knowledge to students and command respect 
is the essential qualification for teachers.
command respect使人肃然起敬，使人敬仰

qualification资格，条件，限制 quality品质，质量

inculcate 灌输，谆谆教诲

我们努力给年轻人灌输公民观念
instill 逐渐灌输
instill high moral values 灌输高尚道德观
inspiration 灵感
give  the students inspiration

methodology 方法学，方法论 methodology 读音并不是method然后ology而是meth然后dology 到了zhei

Teaching methodology vary according to the topic. 教学方法因题而异。
according to 根据，依据

adapt to sth. 使适合，使适应

身体自行调节适应温度变化
