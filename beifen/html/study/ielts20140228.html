One principle I have learned from the autobiography
 of Benjamin Franklin is that the only way to succeed in your 
 business is arriving at moral perfection at the same time.
This is another instance of the truth of an old maxim I had
learned, which says, "He that has once done you a kindness
will be more ready to do you another, than he whom yourself
have obliged."
